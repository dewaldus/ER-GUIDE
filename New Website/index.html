About Us

Built by ER experts,
for South African businesses

Founded in 2016 in Stellenbosch, ERGuide was built to solve a real problem: managers and HR professionals in South Africa need practical, accessible employment relations guidance — but traditional resources are often slow, expensive, or hard to apply in real situations.

Our platform delivers the full body of South African employment relations knowledge in a structured, searchable, and always-current format. From a first disciplinary warning to a Labour Court appearance, ERGuide is the expert resource your team needs.
